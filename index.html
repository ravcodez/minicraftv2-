Minicraft

A Minecraft-inspired voxel replica with world generation, first-person controls, block placement, mining, inventory, day/night cycle, and simple mob behavior.

Controls

  • W/A/S/D: move
  • Space: jump
  • Mouse: look
  • Left click: mine block
  • Right click: place block
  • 1-6: switch block type
